The Straw Hat Grand Fleet[1] is a fleet formed by seven pirate crews who vowed to serve under the Straw Hat Pirates.
The Fleet's members were allies of Monkey D. Luffy during the Dressrosa Arc, and the central characters from The Stories of the Self-Proclaimed Straw Hat Grand Fleet Cover Page Serial.
